# Major / subject / Education topic

## Topic 1 : Special Subject: Math / Art

Five Points can be discussed:

    1. Required/ compulsory subject (Math or some major)
    2. Form the basics of = lay a solid foundation for 
    3. be widely used in other realms
    4. solve problem = problem-soving ability || ability for feeling
    5. calculation +logic thinking || expression of emotion
## Topic 2 : School /University

Three Points can be discussed:

    1. The resource of Teachers (Elite Teachers)
    2. Method of teaching
    3. Management

## The words can be used 

1. well-rounded education ：素质教育
2. exam-oriented education ：应试教育
3. elite : 精英
4. n + with a top reputation : 具有顶尖声誉的
5. think outside the box : 跳出固有思维
6. prerequisite : 前提条件
7. learn sth. by rote: 对某事死记硬背
8. learn sth. through understanding : 理解性的记忆
9. procrastination : 拖延症
10. slack off : 懈怠
11. supervise : 监管 （monitor 同样有监管意，在education话题中最好不要使用）
12. after-school activity = extra-curricular activity
13. provide 提供

# After Class
## The words for the Education topic

1. sth be an arduous process 是一个艰难的/费力的过程
   
   eg. attendinga university with a top reputation often is an arduous but rewarding process
2. be very rewarding

    eg. teaching can be a very rewarding career. it allows teachers to have an input in the development of young lives.

3. someone's aspiration/pursuit 梦想
    
    eg. the education aspirations / pursuits of rural youth tend to be different from those of students living elsewhere

4. concentrate on sth 集中精力于某事

5. form the basis of sth 为某事打下基础
6. use lateral thinking 
7. rely on linear thinking 依赖于直线型的思维，受到思维的局限
8. break the mould 打破常规
9. academic performance 学术表现
10. academic qualifications 学历
11. be gifted / be talented in有天赋的
12. rote memorization 重复记忆，机械记忆=learn sth by rote
13. read extensively 广泛地阅读
14. induce cramming 导致考前突击
15. ignorance 缺乏对于某科目的了解，对某科目的无知
16. self-discipline 自制力
17. role model 榜样
18. meet sb’s expectation 达到某人的期望
19. cover a lot of ground （在课程或科目中）覆盖了很广泛的领域
20. be well-rounded (某人)爱好和经历都是很广泛的
21. self-paced learning/ education 学生可以自己控制进度的学习
22. specialize in sth 专供某个领域
23. extra-curricular activities 课外活动
24. promote equality 促进（学生之间的）平等
25. repress individuality 压制个性
26. distinguish sb/ sth from 区分人或事物之间的差异

    importantly, our commonly held feelings can help distinguish us from other cultures. 重要的是，我们彼此共同拥有的情感能帮忙我们从其它的文化中将自己分辨出来。

27. anti-social behavior 对他人和社会有负面影响的行为

28. be well-behaved 行为得体的

29. adolescence 青春期

    the book deals with the joys and sorrows of adolescence. 这本书讲述了伴着青春时光所到来的甜美和忧伤。 

30. drop out of school 辍学

    in this country, each year many students are forced to drop out of school. 在这个国家中，每年都有许多学生被迫退学。 

31. skip school 逃学

32. be bilingual 双语的 

    donna appreciates she had the chance to be bilingual. donna很感激她能获得了能学会两种语言的机会。 

33. be monolingual 单一语种的

34. slack off 学习或工作不努力，懈怠

35. disruptive students 不遵守纪律的学生

    his teacher described him as a disruptive students—always distracting other children. 他的老师把他形容为一个总是干扰其他人的不守纪律的学生。

36. unruly students 不听话的学生

37. meet the demand of 满足某种需求

38. inform sb about sth 让某人了解到某方面的信息

    studying geography informs us about the different kinds of relationships that develop between a particulate environment and the people who live there.  学习地理可以让我们了解到特定的环境与其中所生活的人类间关系。

39. put theory into practice 把理论运用到实践中去

    the workshop helps students to put theory into practice. they can test their ideas or plans for practical application there. 研讨会可以帮助学生更好地让理论结合实际。在那里他们可以通过实际操作来检验自己的想法和计划。

40. first-hand experience 第一手经验，最直接的体验

    by doing experiments, students acquired first-hand experience with the periodic table of the chemical elements. 
    通过亲手操作实验，学生得到了对于元素周期表的第一手经验。

41. the responsibility for sth falls on sb 对某人肩负着做某事的责任

    the responsibility for educating children used to fall both on parents and on teachers. 在过去，往往是家长和老师共同肩负着教育孩子的责任。
42. the responsibility for sth  rests with sb. 某种责任应该由某人承担

    the responsibility for limiting children’s access to violence on tv rests with the parents.  家长应承担让孩子远离电视中暴力行为的责任。

43. shirk one’s responsibility for sth 某人逃避某种责任

44. fulfill one’s responsibility for 尽到责任

45. deny sb the freedom to do sth 不给某人在某方面的自由

    there is no reason to deny students the freedom to choose subjects that interest and motivate them. 剥夺孩子选择自己喜欢事物的自由是不明智的。

46. required subject=compulsory subject 必修科目

    it is recognized as an important compulsory subject throughout schooling. 它在整个学校教育体系中均被认为是一门很重要的必修课。

47. kindle one’s interest in sth 唤起某人对某事物的兴趣

    the students’ interest in maths was kindled by emma’s excellent teaching. emma 用她精湛的教学技巧重新燃起了学生们对数学的兴趣。

48. optional subject/ course 选修课

49. there is no substitute for sb/ sth 某人／ 事物是无法替代的

    there is no substitute for a face-to face relationship with a caring and well-informed teacher. 传统的面对面式教育是无可替代的。

50. distance learning 远程教育

    the biggest advantage of distance learning programs is their accessibility. 远程教育的最大优势就是它可以随时随地让我们开始学习

51. compromise 

1）考虑各种不同需求之后的折中产物 
2）以牺牲某种因素为代价

    1)	all classroom learning is, in fact, a compromise between the different needs of various students. 所有的课堂学习实际上都是在不同学生的不同学习需求上这种而成的产物。
    2）the modification might compromise safety in some way. 某种意义上这些改动将可能牺牲安全因素。

52. be sb’s mentor 某人的良师益友

53. sb’s peer 同龄人

54. burn the midnight oil （为了突进完成某项任务而）学习或工作到深夜

    amy has to get the report finished by tomorrow so she’ll be burning the midnight oil tonight. 由于明天是amy交报告的最后期限，今晚她必须要熬夜工作了

55. study intensively 高强度学习
